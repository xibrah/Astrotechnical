Refueling Economics

Delivery Vehicle: Rocket Lab Electron delivers 160-180 kg of actual propellant per flight to sun-synchronous orbit (after accounting for tankage and hardware).

Service Capacity: Sufficient to refuel multiple small satellites or one larger spacecraft per delivery mission, enabling extended mission life and active orbital maintenance.

Business Case: Growing datacenter satellite constellations and Earth observation networks require reliable in-orbit refueling infrastructure. Sun-sync orbit is increasingly crowded with commercial and government birds needing stationkeeping propellant.

Alternative: Momentus Vigoride Flying Depot

Photon spacecraft bus configured as mobile propellant depot with heritage avionics, power, and propulsion - potentially more mass-efficient than custom tankage.
